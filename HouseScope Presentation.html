üí° Why I Built This

My name is Chawanangwa Nkosi, a BSocSci student at the University of Cape Town studying Information Systems & Industrial Sociology.

When I was looking for accommodation in Cape Town last year, my parents and I were shocked. Coming from Centurion, Pretoria where housing is much cheaper, the Cape Town market felt completely out of reach.

This made me question: How do people know if they can actually afford a home in different SA cities? Is there a tool that shows the REAL cost, not just glossy property ads?

I searched. Nothing existed. So I built HouseScope.

üöÄ Key Features

  • Full Cost Breakdown: See ALL monthly costs, not just the bond
  • DTI Ratio Analysis: Know if banks will approve you (under 35% = excellent)
  • Location-Based Pricing: Costs vary by city (Cape Town vs Durban vs Joburg)
  • Path to Affordability: Shows exactly how to qualify (reduce expenses OR increase income)
  • Smart Recommendations: Personalized tips (FLISP subsidy, cheaper areas, co-buying)
  • Visual Charts: See principal vs interest breakdown clearly
  • PDF Reports: Download and share your affordability analysis
  • 100% Private: No data stored, instant calculations
